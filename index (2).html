Campus Life

The School

Was established in 1974

Teaches over 100 children per year and was awarded the Best School of the Year Reward in 2013.



School Library

Was origionaly added to the school in 1980

It was completly renovated in 2001. There are only two books that are still in the library from the begining of the library. All of them were replased when it got renovated.



School Cafeteria

Was built in 1975

Has over 600 seats and 60 tables, and the biggest variety of meal choices in the world. Has about 45 meal options, as much as a resturaunt menu.
